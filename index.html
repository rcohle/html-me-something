Rosanna's Digital Art

Since childhood, I've enjoyed drawing. When I became comfortable using a computer, I started using graphics programs to create digital artwork. The main program I create art in is the Gimp, which is a free graphics program. I have also used Paint Shop Pro 7 and Microsoft Paint.

I believe anyone with curiosity and a bit of time can make digital artwork! There are tons of video and text tutorials out there. The concept of layers has taken my digital artwork to a more sophisticated level.
